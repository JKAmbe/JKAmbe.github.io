Nightmare Slayers
A collaboration between me and 3 other game design students during my last semester at UTS, showcased during the 2023 UTS tech fest. The pitch was to make a starfox 64 style 2 axis shoot'em'up' but with multiple playable character and our own twist of style.

I played multiple role during the game's production. I managed tickets, programmed a LOT, and I even get to draw and model my own characters. A lot happened as always; getting burned out, personal issues, technical problems but we got there in the end and we're all VERY proud with how it turned out.

Oh, and the best part? IT CAN BE PLAYED AS A STANDALONE VR GAME (download file available on the Itch.io page)
